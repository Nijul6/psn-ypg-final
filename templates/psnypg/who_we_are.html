Who We Are

Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria – Young Pharmacists Group (PSN-YPG)

The Young Pharmacists Group (YPG) is a dynamic interest group under the umbrella of the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), comprising pharmacists who are either 35 years of age or younger or have graduated from an accredited pharmacy school within the last five years. Since its inception, PSN-YPG has become a driving force for positive change, empowering young pharmacists to actively shape Nigeria’s healthcare landscape while fostering personal and professional growth.

At Psn-ypg, we are your trusted healthcare partner, making sure you receive the best care possible for a healthier tomorrow.

Our Mission


To develop young pharmacists into agents of positive change at the local, national, and international levels by equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and network necessary to make meaningful contributions to healthcare and society.


Our Vision


1. To cultivate a growing community of young pharmacists actively contributing to the improvement of Nigeria’s healthcare system.

2. To ensure the continuous development of pharmacy as an indispensable profession, occupying a unique and impactful role in healthcare delivery.

3. To promote pharmacy education and practice through socially responsible, proactive, innovative, and culturally competent leaders.
