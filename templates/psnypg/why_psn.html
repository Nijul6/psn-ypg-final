Why PSN-YPG Matters

Young pharmacists represent the future of Nigeria’s healthcare system. By providing a platform that nurtures their potential,
PSN-YPG contributes to:

Improved Delivery

Empowering pharmacists with the skills and confidence to implement impactful changes in their workplaces and communities.

Innovation and Research

Encouraging young minds to explore cutting-edge solutions to Nigeria’s health challenges.

Policy Influence

Ensuring that young pharmacists’ perspectives are considered in shaping the policies that affect their profession and healthcare delivery at large..
