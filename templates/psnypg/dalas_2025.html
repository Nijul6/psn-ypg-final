DALA 2025: Our Flagship Event

The PSN-YPG Annual National Conference is our flagship event, drawing pharmacists from across Nigeria and beyond. The upcoming DALA 2025 conference, themed:

“Politics, Policies, and Pills: Young Pharmacists at the Intersection of Governance and Medicine Security in Nigeria”

…promises to be a landmark event. Delegates will gain insights into the evolving role of pharmacists in shaping healthcare policies while connecting with industry leaders and exploring new innovations.

This event is more than just a gathering — it’s a launchpad for young pharmacists to become catalysts for change in Nigeria’s healthcare system.

Why Partner with PSN-YPG?

Supporting PSN-YPG isn’t just a sponsorship — it’s an investment in Nigeria’s healthcare future. As a partner, your organization will:

  1. Gain Direct Access to a specialized audience of young, talented pharmacists eager to embrace new ideas and innovations.
  2. Showcase Your Brand as a champion of healthcare development and youth empowerment.
  3. Contribute to Policy and Innovation by supporting meaningful discussions that shape Nigeria’s healthcare policies and pharmaceutical practices.
  4. Enjoy Enhanced Visibility through conference materials, speaking opportunities, and direct engagement with delegates.
Let’s Shape the Future Together

At PSN-YPG, we believe young pharmacists are not just participants in Nigeria’s healthcare system — they are its future leaders. Join us in equipping the next generation of pharmacists with the tools, knowledge, and confidence they need to make a lasting impact.

Our Mission


To develop young pharmacists into agents of positive change at the local, national, and international levels by equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and network necessary to make meaningful contributions to healthcare and society.


Our Vision


1. To cultivate a growing community of young pharmacists actively contributing to the improvement of Nigeria’s healthcare system.

2. To ensure the continuous development of pharmacy as an indispensable profession, occupying a unique and impactful role in healthcare delivery.

3. To promote pharmacy education and practice through socially responsible, proactive, innovative, and culturally competent leaders.

4. To foster inclusivity and participation of young pharmacists across Nigeria, regardless of state of origin, socio-economic background, gender, or other factors.
