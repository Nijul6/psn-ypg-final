Our Mission

To develop young pharmacists into agents of positive change at the local, national, and international levels by equipping them with the skills, knowledge, and network necessary to make meaningful contributions to healthcare and society.

Our Vision

To cultivate a growing community of young pharmacists actively contributing to the improvement of Nigeria’s healthcare system.

To ensure the continuous development of pharmacy as an indispensable profession, occupying a unique and impactful role in healthcare delivery.

Core Value

To be the leading pharmacy in our community, recognized for our commitment to health, innovation, and customer care. We aspire to empower individuals to make informed healthcare choices, promoting wellness and ensuring better outcomes for all.

Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria – Young Pharmacists Group (PSN-YPG)

About PSN-YPG

The Young Pharmacists Group (YPG) is a vibrant interest group under the Pharmaceutical Society of Nigeria (PSN), dedicated to empowering pharmacists aged 35 and below, or within five years of graduation. We equip young pharmacists with the skills, knowledge, and network they need to shape the future of healthcare in Nigeria and beyond.
