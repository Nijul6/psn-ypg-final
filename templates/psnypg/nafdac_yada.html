About YADA

The Youth Against Drug Abuse (YADA) Project is a school-based drug prevention initiative that equips young Nigerians with knowledge, resources, and support to resist drug abuse. Led by PSN-YPG in partnership with NAFDAC, YADA combines education, mentorship, and digital outreach to tackle the rising crisis of youth drug use.

8 States | 54,000 + Students | 5,400 Parents & Teachers
40% projected reduction in drug use among participating schools

    Our Goals
  1. Prevent drug abuse through school-based education
  2. Empower students & parents with knowledge & support
  3. Use digital tools for real-time engagement & crisis intervention
    How It Works
  1. Peer-Led Interventions
  2. Prevention Education
  3. Digital Support Services
  4. Policy & Data-Driven Advocacy
