NAFDAC-YADA Phase II 2025

About YADA

The Youth Against Drug Abuse (YADA) Project is a school-based drug prevention initiative that equips young Nigerians with knowledge, resources, and support to resist drug abuse. Led by PSN-YPG in partnership with NAFDAC, YADA combines education, mentorship, and digital outreach to tackle the rising crisis of youth drug use.

8 states | 54,000+ students | 5,400 parents & teachers
40% projected reduction in drug use among participating schools

    Our Goals
  1. ✅ Prevent drug abuse through school-based education
  2. Location: FABs Event Center, Kano State
  3. ✅ Empower students & parents with knowledge & support
  4. ✅ Use digital tools for real-time engagement & crisis intervention
How It Works

1️⃣ Prevention Education
2️⃣ Peer-Led Interventions
    Structured school sessions on:
  1. Drug awareness & resistance skills
  2. Peer pressure management
  3. Impact of social media on substance abuse
2️⃣ Peer-Led Interventions
  1. 👥 YADA Ambassadors: Students trained as anti-drug role models
  2. 📢 Social media campaigns: Spreading awareness among 100,000+ youths
3️⃣ Digital Support Services
  1. 🌐 YADA Resource Center (website with educational tools & resources)
  2. 💬 24/7 Real-Time Chatbot (connect with pharmacists & psychologists)
  3. 📞 Toll-Free Helpline (confidential support & referrals)
4️⃣ Policy & Data-Driven Advocacy
  1. 📊 Baseline & impact surveys to track progress
  2. 💬 24/7 Real-Time Chatbot (connect with pharmacists & psychologists)
  3. 📌 Conference presentations to influence national drug prevention policies
