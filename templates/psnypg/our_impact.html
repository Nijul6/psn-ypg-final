Our Impact

Since its inception, PSN-YPG has made remarkable strides in uniting young pharmacists across Nigeria and beyond. Through our programs and initiatives, we have:

  1. Empowered Hundreds of Young Pharmacists: Providing them with access to professional development opportunities, mentorship, and the tools to excel in their careers.
  2. Fostered Cross-Country Collaboration: Building a robust network of young pharmacists who share knowledge and experiences to tackle common challenges in healthcare.
  3. Sparked Policy Discussions: Advocating for greater representation of young voices in shaping pharmaceutical policies and practices at local, national, and international levels.
  4. Promoted Public Health Initiatives: Organizing community-driven events, such as the Young Pharmacists Marathon Against Drug Abuse (MADA), aimed at raising awareness of public health issues and promoting healthier lifestyles
