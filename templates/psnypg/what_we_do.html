What We Do

PSN-YPG is committed to shaping the future of pharmacy in Nigeria by creating platforms for collaboration, education, and innovation. Our key initiatives include:

  1. Annual National Conference: A melting pot of ideas where young pharmacists engage in seminars, workshops, and scientific discussions.
  2. Capacity Building Programs: Training sessions focused on leadership, innovation, and emerging healthcare trends
  3. Advocacy and Policy Engagement: Amplifying the voice of young pharmacists in policy discussions that shape Nigeria’s healthcare future.
  4. Community Outreach: Programs like the Young Pharmacists Marathon Against Drug Abuse (MADA) aim to raise awareness and fight public health challenges.
  5. Networking and Mentorship: Facilitating connections between young pharmacists and seasoned professionals to guide career growth and innovation.
